## Short description
A camera-lucida tracing companion for artists. Your reference, ghosted over your paper.

## Full description
Resonance Lantern turns your device into a tracing lantern: load a
reference image, see it ghosted over your camera's live view of the
paper, and draw what you see — the old camera lucida, reborn gently.

• Ghost overlay with opacity-as-comfort — fade the reference until it
  guides without commanding
• Zoom the image and the view independently; alignment frame
• Practice outlines with real curves, by category and difficulty —
  or roll the dice
• Paper mode when you'd rather trace from the screen directly
• Projector mode: your reference on pure black for projecting onto
  canvas — zoom, pan, rotate, invert for dark surfaces, keep-awake
• A gentle session journal — minutes practiced, never judged.
  No streaks. No shame.

Built with love, first for one poet-artist's hands — the pride palette
is not a theme option; it is the light source.

Sovereign by architecture: data collected — none. No account, no cloud.
Camera frames never leave your device — the overlay is live view only,
nothing records without you pressing capture. Export your journal as
open JSON; the purge truly purges.
