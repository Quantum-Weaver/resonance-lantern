## What this is
**Resonance Lantern** — the tutor. "The place you learn, step by step,
light in hand." Third of the original triad (Echoes looks back, Compass
looks around, **Lantern looks forward**). Formerly **CanvasGuide**; named by
the Council 2026-07-03 — the naming ceremony and canonical definition are
**Kimi's catalog-entry-011** (`resonance-kimi/catalog-entry-011.md`), which
is the SSOT: interactive learning platform, guides vessels through complex
processes with visual clarity, one step at a time. *Not* a beacon, path,
atlas, or manual — the light you carry.
